Settings:

User should be able to set their default units: pounds or kilos

Locations:
When creating a new location, there should be a "lbs" | "kgs" toggle next to the "new" and next to the "current" location. it should default to the value from user settings. This makes it easy for people who travel to other gyms which have equipment in different units.

History:

the history tab should have a couple views -- a "feed" view and a calendar view.

The feed view is just an "endless scroll" feed with cards with summaries of all the workouts you've done, divided by the months. The summary should say the name of the workout, and what percentage of the prescribed sets were done, and have a "show details" button that expands the card to show more details.

The calendar view shows an "endless scroll" feed of month calendars with some indicator on the dates whether workouts were performed on that date or not. tapping on the date opens the same workout details from the "feed" view.

the "strong" app does this very well.

Progress:

I'm now adding a "progress" tab to the footer. By default, it uses charts to show your progress over time for the first lift of each day in the routine -- for example, Bench is the first lift on one of the days, shoulder press on another, squat on another, etc. Then you can add or remove lifts to the graph to see progress.

The units shown here should reflect user settings
